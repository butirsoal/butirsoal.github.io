29. tentukan turunan dari beberapa fungsi trigonometri berikut


[tex]\sf1.~Turunan~dari~f(x)=\dfrac{cos~x}{sin~x+cos~x}~adalah~\boxed{\sf f'(x)=-\dfrac{1}{1+sin\:2x}}.[/tex]

[tex]\sf2.~Turunan~dari~f(x)=\dfrac{cos~x}{x}~adalah~\boxed{\sf f'(x)=-\dfrac{x~sin~x+cos~x}{x^2}}.[/tex]

[tex]\sf3.~Turunan~dari~f(x)=\dfrac{x^2+1}{x~sin~x}~adalah~\boxed{\sf f'(x)=\dfrac{x^2~sin~x-x^3~cos~x-sin~x-x~cos~x}{x^2~sin^2x}}.[/tex]

ㅤ

ㅤ

PEMBAHASAN

Berikut beberapa rumus dalam turunan fungsi aljabar:

[tex]\boxed{\begin{array}{c|c}\underline{\sf~~~~~~f(x)~~~~~~}&\underline{\sf~~~~~~f'(x)~~~~~~}\\\\\sf k&\sf0\\\\\sf ax&\sf a\\\\\sf ax^n&\sf n\cdot ax^{n-1}\\\\\sf u+v&\sf u'+v'\\\\\sf u-v&\sf u'-v'\\\\\sf u\cdot v&\sf u'v+uv'\\\\\sf\dfrac{u}{v}&\sf\dfrac{u'v-uv'}{v^2}\\\\\sf u^n&\sf n\cdot u^{n-1}\cdot u'\\\\\sf\sqrt{u}&\sf\dfrac{u'}{2\sqrt{u}}\\\sf\end{array}}[/tex]

ㅤ

Adapun rumus untuk turunan trigonometri antara lain:

[tex]\boxed{\begin{array}{c|c}\underline{\sf~~~~~~f(x)~~~~~~}&\underline{\sf~~~~~~f'(x)~~~~~~}\\\\\sf sin\:x&\sf cos\:x\\\\\sf cos\:x&\sf-sin\:x\\\\\sf tan\:x&\sf sec^2x\\\\\sf csc\:x&\sf-csc\:x\cdot cot\:x\\\\\sf sec\:x&\sf sec\:x\cdot tan\:x\\\\\sf cot\:x&\sf-csc^2x\\\\\sf sin\:[f(x)]&\sf f'(x)\cdot cos\:[f(x)]\\\\\sf cos\:[f(x)]&\sf-f'(x)\cdot sin\:[f(x)]\\\\\sf tan\:[f(x)]&\sf f'(x)\cdot sec^2[f(x)]\\\sf\end{array}}[/tex]

ㅤ

[tex]\sf f(x)=\dfrac{cos~x}{sin~x+cos~x}[/tex]

Jawab:

[tex]\sf f(x)=\dfrac{cos~x}{sin~x+cos~x}[/tex]

ㅤ[tex]\sf u=cos~x\implies u'=-sin~x[/tex]

ㅤ[tex]\sf v=sin~x+cos~x\implies v'=cos~x-sin~x[/tex]

[tex]\sf f'(x)=\dfrac{u'v-uv'}{v^2}[/tex]

[tex]\sf f'(x)=\dfrac{(-sin~x)(sin~x+cos~x)-(cos~x)(cos~x-sin~x)}{(sin~x+cos~x)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\sf f'(x)=\dfrac{-sin^2x-sin~x\cdot cos~x-cos^2x+sin~x\cdot cos~x)}{sin^2x+2sin~x\cdot cos~x+cos^2x}[/tex]

[tex]\sf f'(x)=\dfrac{-sin^2x-cos^2x-sin~x\cdot cos~x+sin~x\cdot cos~x)}{sin^2x+sin\:2x+cos^2x}[/tex]

[tex]\sf f'(x)=\dfrac{-(sin^2x+cos^2x)}{sin^2x+cos^2x+sin\:2x}[/tex]

[tex]\sf f'(x)=-\dfrac{sin^2x+cos^2x}{sin^2x+cos^2x+sin\:2x}[/tex]

[tex]\sf f'(x)=-\dfrac{1}{1+sin\:2x}[/tex]

ㅤ

[tex]\sf Jadi~turunan~dari~f(x)=\dfrac{cos~x}{sin~x+cos~x}~adalah~\boxed{\sf f'(x)=-\dfrac{1}{1+sin\:2x}}.[/tex]

38. Tentukan turunan tiap fungsi trigonometri berikut terhadap x. F(x) =X²cotan 2x​


Turunan fungsi trigonometri f(x) = x² cotan 2x terhadap x adalah 2x · cotan 2x - x² · cosec² 2x atau dalam bentuk lain x(2 cotan 2x - x cosec² 2x).

Pembahasan

Soal di atas dapat diselesaikan dengan rumus berikut.

Jika terdapat fungsi: y = u · v

Maka turunannya: y' = u' · v + v' · u

↓↓↓

f(x) = x² cotan 2x

Berarti

u = x² → u' = 2x

v = cotan 2x → v' = -cosec² 2x

Maka

f'(x) = u' · v + v' · u

f'(x) = 2x · cotan 2x + (-cosec² 2x) · x²

f'(x) = 2x · cotan 2x - x² · cosec² 2x

f'(x) = x(2 cotan 2x - x cosec² 2x)

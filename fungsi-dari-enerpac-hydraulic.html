The fuel oil injection system for a diesel engine  Delivering right amount of fuel: The function of the fuel injection system is to provide the right amount of fuel at the right moment and in a suitable condition for the combustion process. There must therefore be some form of measured fuel supply, a means of timing the delivery and the atomisation of the fuel. The injection of the fuel is achieved by the location of cams on a camshaft. This camshaft rotates at engine speed for a two-stroke engine and at half engine speed for a four-stroke. There are two basic systems in use, each of which employs a combination of mechanical and hydraulic operations. The most common system is the jerk pump; the other is the common rail. A typical fuel injector is shown in Figure , It can be seen to be two basic parts, the nozzle and the nozzle holder or body. The high-pressure fuel enters and travels down a passage in the body and then into a passage in the nozzle, ending finally in a chamber surrounding the needle valve.  The needle valve is held closed on a mitred seat by an intermediate spindle and a spring in the injector body. The spring pressure, and hence the injector opening pressure, can be set by a compression nut which acts on the spring. The nozzle and injector body are manufactured as a matching pair and are accurately ground to give a good oil seal. The two are joined by a nozzle nut. The needle valve will open when the fuel pressure acting on the needle valve tapered face exerts a sufficient force to overcome the spring compression. The fuel then flows into a lower chamber and is forced out through a series of tiny holes. The small holes are sized and arranged to atomise, or break into tiny drops, all of the fuel oil, which will then readily burn. Once the injector pump or timing valve cuts off the high pressure fuel supply the needle valve will shut quickly under the spring compression force. All slow-speed two-stroke engines and many medium-speed fourstroke engines are now operated almost continuously on heavy fuel. A fuel circulating system is therefore necessary and this is usually arranged within the fuel injector. During injection the high-pressure fuel will open the circulation valve for injection to take place. When the engine is stopped the fuel booster pump supplies fuel which the circulation valve directs around the injector body.

12. Sebuah dongkrak hydraulic diameter silinder A 25 mm dan B 350 mm. Massa kendaraan 1,7 metrik ton. Tentukan besarnya gaya minimal pada tuas sehingga kendaraan bisa terangkat (dalam Newton)


Jawaban:

Untuk menentukan besarnya gaya minimal yang diperlukan untuk mengangkat kendaraan dengan dongkrak hidrolik, kita dapat menggunakan prinsip dasar hukum Pascal yang menyatakan bahwa tekanan dalam fluida dalam wadah tertutup akan merata dalam semua arah. Oleh karena itu, tekanan yang diberikan oleh dongkrak hidrolik pada silinder A akan sama dengan tekanan yang dihasilkan pada silinder B.

Mari kita hitung tekanan pada silinder A:

Tekanan (P) = gaya (F) / luas permukaan (A)

Dalam hal ini, kita ingin mengangkat kendaraan yang memiliki massa 1,7 metrik ton atau 1.700 kg. Percepatan gravitasi bumi adalah sekitar 9,8 m/s².

F = massa x percepatan gravitasi

F = 1700 kg x 9,8 m/s² = 16.660 N

Selanjutnya, kita akan menghitung tekanan pada silinder A:

Luas permukaan silinder A (A) = π x (jari-jari)^2

A = π x (0,025 m)^2 = π x 0,000625 m² = 0,001963495 m²

Tekanan pada silinder A = F / A

Tekanan pada silinder A = 16.660 N / 0,001963495 m² ≈ 8.486.835,1 N/m² atau 8,49 MPa (Mega Pascal)

Karena tekanan di silinder A harus sama dengan tekanan di silinder B, maka gaya yang diberikan oleh dongkrak pada silinder B juga sekitar 16.660 N.

Jadi, besarnya gaya minimal pada tuas untuk mengangkat kendaraan adalah sekitar 16.660 Newton.

21. apa manfaat dari electro-hydraulic sistem?​


Jawaban:

Sistem electro-hydraulic adalah sistem yang menggabungkan teknologi hidrolik dan teknologi elektronik. Sistem ini memiliki beberapa manfaat, antara lain:

1. **Presisi**: Sistem electro-hydraulic dapat memberikan presisi yang tinggi dalam mengontrol gerakan mekanisme dengan pengontrolan tekanan.

2. **Fleksibilitas**: Sistem ini dapat bergerak bebas ketika diberikan tekanan dan flow control valve dapat dipakai.

3. **Mampu memindahkan tenaga besar**: Meskipun menggunakan komponen yang relatif kecil, sistem electro-hydraulic dapat memindahkan tenaga yang besar.

4. **Meningkatkan efisiensi**: Sistem ini dapat meningkatkan efisiensi dalam penggunaan energi karena dapat mengontrol tekanan dan aliran fluida secara akurat.

5. **Meningkatkan keamanan**: Sistem electro-hydraulic dapat meningkatkan keamanan karena dapat mengontrol gerakan mekanisme dengan presisi dan dapat mengurangi risiko kecelakaan kerja.

6. **Meningkatkan kenyamanan**: Sistem ini dapat meningkatkan kenyamanan karena dapat mengontrol gerakan mekanisme dengan presisi dan dapat mengurangi getaran dan kebisingan.

Dalam industri, sistem electro-hydraulic banyak digunakan pada aplikasi dengan daya tinggi hingga operasi peralatan yang membutuhkan presisi dan fleksibilitas.

Pembahasan

Teks rumpang di atas adalah termasuk jenis teks Procedure. Procedure text adalah teks yang berfungsi untuk memberitahu cara membuat atau cara mengoperasikan sesuatu.

Social Function dari Procedure Text: To tell how to make or how to operate something.

Generic Structure dari Procedure text adalah:

1. Goal: Berisi apa yang akan dibuat atau dioperasikan (Judul teks). Biasanya diawali dengan How to....

2. Materials: Berisi alat dan bahan yang dibutuhkan. Namun bagian ini biasanya tidak ada untuk jenis procedure text cara pengoperasian sesuatu.

3. Steps: Berisi langkah-langkah yang harus dilakukan untuk membuat dan mengoperasikan sesuatu.

Language features dari Procedure text adalah:

• menggunakan adverbial of sequence.

• menggunakan imperative sentence.

• menggunakan action verbs.

• menggunakan simple present tense.

• menggunakan adverb of manner.

The fuel oil injection system for a diesel engine  Delivering right amount of fuel: The function of the fuel injection system is to provide the right amount of fuel at the right moment and in a suitable condition for the combustion process. There must therefore be some form of measured fuel supply, a means of timing the delivery and the atomisation of the fuel. The injection of the fuel is achieved by the location of cams on a camshaft. This camshaft rotates at engine speed for a two-stroke engine and at half engine speed for a four-stroke. There are two basic systems in use, each of which employs a combination of mechanical and hydraulic operations. The most common system is the jerk pump; the other is the common rail. A typical fuel injector is shown in Figure , It can be seen to be two basic parts, the nozzle and the nozzle holder or body. The high-pressure fuel enters and travels down a passage in the body and then into a passage in the nozzle, ending finally in a chamber surrounding the needle valve.  The needle valve is held closed on a mitred seat by an intermediate spindle and a spring in the injector body. The spring pressure, and hence the injector opening pressure, can be set by a compression nut which acts on the spring. The nozzle and injector body are manufactured as a matching pair and are accurately ground to give a good oil seal. The two are joined by a nozzle nut. The needle valve will open when the fuel pressure acting on the needle valve tapered face exerts a sufficient force to overcome the spring compression. The fuel then flows into a lower chamber and is forced out through a series of tiny holes. The small holes are sized and arranged to atomise, or break into tiny drops, all of the fuel oil, which will then readily burn. Once the injector pump or timing valve cuts off the high pressure fuel supply the needle valve will shut quickly under the spring compression force. All slow-speed two-stroke engines and many medium-speed fourstroke engines are now operated almost continuously on heavy fuel. A fuel circulating system is therefore necessary and this is usually arranged within the fuel injector. During injection the high-pressure fuel will open the circulation valve for injection to take place. When the engine is stopped the fuel booster pump supplies fuel which the circulation valve directs around the injector body.

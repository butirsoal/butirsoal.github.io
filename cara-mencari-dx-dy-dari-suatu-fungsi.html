3. carilah dy/dx dari fungsi y=log x⁵​


[tex]\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{d}{dx}(\log{x^5})[/tex]

Mengubah log ke ln :

[tex]\log(x^5)=5\log x\\\\\log(x^5)=5\times (\frac{^e\log x}{^e\log10})\\\\\log(x^5)=5\times (\frac{\ln x}{\ln10})\\[/tex]

Sehingga :

[tex]\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{d}{dx}(\log{x^5})\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{d}{dx}(\frac{5\ln{x}}{\ln10}) = \frac{5}{\ln10}\times\frac{d}{dx}(\ln{x})\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{5}{\ln10}\times \frac{1}{x}\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{5}{x\ln{(10)}}[/tex]

4. cari nilai dy/dx dari fungsi y= (1-2x^2)^3


Dasar teori: Aturan rantai turunan

Misalkan:
[tex]
u=x^2\\
v=1-2u\\
y=v^3
[/tex]

Maka
[tex]
\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{dy}{dv}×\frac{dv}{du}× \frac{du}{dx}\\
=\frac{d(v^3)}{dv}×\frac{d(1-2u)}{du}×\frac{d(x^2)}{dx}\\
=3v^2×(-2)×2x\\
=3(1-2x^2)×(-4x) \\
=-12x(1-2x^2)
[/tex]

Silakan dicek perhitungannya.

33. cari dy/dx dari fungsi : y = ( x4 + 2x)( x3 + 2x2 + 1)


• DiferentiaL

we have a function :

[tex]y = ( {x}^{4} + 2x)( {x}^{3} + 2 {x}^{2} + 1)[/tex]

Let :

[tex]u = {x}^{4} + 2x = > \frac{du}{dx} = 4 {x}^{3} + 2[/tex]

[tex]v = {x}^{3} + 2 {x}^{2} + 1 = > \frac{dv}{dx} = 3 {x}^{2} + 4x[/tex]

Use the form of differential , hence :

[tex] \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{du}{dx} \: . \: v + u \: . \: \frac{dv}{dx} \\ \frac{dy}{dx} = (4 {x}^{3} + 2)( {x}^{3} + 2 {x}^{2} + 1) + ( {x}^{4} + 2x)(3 {x}^{2} + 4x) \\ \frac{dy}{dx} = 4 {x}^{6} + 8 {x}^{5} + 4 {x}^{3} + 2 {x}^{3} + 4 {x}^{2} + 2 + 12 {x}^{6} + 4 {x}^{5} + 6 {x}^{3} + 8 {x}^{2} \\ \frac{dy}{dx} =16 {x}^{6} + 12 {x}^{5} + 12 {x}^{3} + 12 {x}^{2} + 2[/tex]

We've done

rich countries can afford to spend money on the environment.

rich mahogany furniture.
▪ generating wealth; valuable:
not all footballers enjoy rich rewards from the game.
2 existing in plentiful quantities; abundant:
the rich flora and fauna of the forest.
▪ having (a particular thing) in large amounts:
many vegetables and fruits are rich in vitamins
[in combination] a protein-rich diet.
▪ (of food) containing a large amount of fat, spices, sugar, etc.:
dishes with wonderfully rich sauces.
▪ (of drink) full-bodied:
a rich and hoppy best bitter.
▪ (of the fuel and air mixture in an internal-combustion engine) containing a high proportion of fuel:
crank the engine until it fires and return the mixture to ‘rich’.
3 producing a large quantity of something:
novels have always been a rich source of material for the film industry.
▪ (of land) having the properties necessary to produce fertile growth:
the city is surrounded by rich agricultural land.
▪ (of a mine or mineral deposit) yielding a large quantity of precious metal:
one of the richest phosphate mines in the West.
4 (of a colour, sound, smell, etc.) pleasantly deep or strong:
his rich bass voice
basmati rice has a rich aroma.
5 interesting because full of variety:
what a full, rich life you lead!
6 informal (of a remark) causing ironic amusement or indignation:
these comments are a bit rich coming from a woman with no money worries.

richness noun

Pembahasan:

Taktik man to man defence adalah penjagaan pemain kunci lawan dengan mengandalkan seorang atau dua orang pemain bertahan untuk mengikuti dan mengawal pemain kunci tersebut kemanapun ia bergerak. Sementara, zona defence adalah penjagaan ketat terhadap wilayah pertahanan sendiri, biasanya dengan menumpuk banyak pemain di area kotak penalti sehingga tim lawan sulit menembusnya. Jika kedua taktik ini dilakukan maka pertahanan akan solid dan sulit ditembus.

Namun kini sepakbola telah berkembang, termasuk dalam hal taktik. Tak heran jika muncul beberapa taktik bertahan baru atau hasil pengembangan yang ada, misalnya

taktik pertahanan garis tinggi yang memberi pressing lawan hingga ke garis tengah. Pola ini biasanya digunakan oleh tim  yang mengandalkan penguasaan bola seperti Barcelona.gegenpressing yang mengandalkan pressing ketat oleh seluruh pemain ke semua pemain lawan yang menguasai bola, termasuk di daerah pertahanan lawan. 28. describe the function of each of these parts in a plat cell-Cell Membrane-Mitochondrion-chloroplast-cell wall-ribosome-circular DNA​


My Answer :

Cell Membrane is also called the plasma membrane, is found in all cells and separates the interior of the cell from the outside environment

function : The main role of the cell membrane is to provide protection to the cell from its surroundings.

Mitochondrion  is an organelle found in the cells of most eukaryotes, such as animals, plants and fungi.

function : generate most of the chemical energy needed to power the cell's biochemical reactions.

Chloroplast is plant cell organelles that convert light energy into relatively stable chemical energy via the photosynthetic process.

function : produce energy through photosynthesis and oxygen-release processes, which sustain plant growth and crop yield.

Cell wall is a structural layer surrounding some types of cells, just outside the cell membrane

function : provide structural strength and support, and also provide a semi-permeable surface for molecules to pass in and out of the cell.

Ribosome is minute particles consisting of RNA and associated proteins

function : synthesize proteins

Circular DNA is DNA that forms a closed loop and has no ends.

function : an important role in cancers by regulating the expression of oncogenes, chromosome accessibility, genome replication, immune response, and cellular communications
